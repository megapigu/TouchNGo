What is GOtransport?

An incentive from the government in collaboration with myRapid and Touch 'n Go to encourage Malaysian citizens to use the public transport more often.

Who is eligible for GOtransport?

Malaysian citizen of any age that has a working TouchnGo eWallet account registered under their IC and phone number.
